FAQ

Questions, answered.

Everything you'd want to know before placing a Numnumshub machine in your space — from cost and contracts to restocking and maintenance.

Is it really free?
+
Yes. There's no cost to host a Numnumshub machine. We cover the machine, the install, the stock, the maintenance, and the payment hardware. You provide the space and a power socket.
How quickly can a machine be installed?
+
Most venues are live within 48 hours of the site survey. We schedule install around your operating hours, so there's no disruption.
Who handles restocking?
+
Our team. We restock on a schedule tuned to your footfall — typically weekly, more often for higher-traffic sites — and rotate the range based on what's actually selling.
What happens if the machine breaks down?
+
One call to us and we're on it. All maintenance, parts, and call-outs are included. Average response time is under four hours during working days.
Do the machines take card and contactless?
+
Every machine is fitted with contactless, Apple Pay, Google Pay, chip-and-pin, and cash. We make sure your people can pay however they prefer.
Can I choose what's stocked?
+
Absolutely. We tailor the product mix to your audience and adjust it over time based on sales data. Want a protein-led range, healthy options, or treat-led? We'll build it together.
How much space does a machine need?
+
A typical machine needs about 1m of wall space and a standard UK power socket. We'll confirm the right footprint during the site survey.
Where do you operate?
+
Across Greater London. From central boroughs to the outer ring — if you're in London, we can serve you.
Is there a long contract?
+
No long lock-ins. We use a simple host agreement that protects both sides, with sensible notice periods. We'd rather earn the spot every cycle than tie you down.
How do I get started?
+
The easiest way is to drop us a quick message or WhatsApp us. We'll book a 10-minute call, then a site survey if it's a fit.
